Intel Sustainability Timeline

2000-2007

Water Conservation

Advanced water recycling systems.

Intel invested heavily in water conservation initiatives and implemented advanced reclamation technologies that reduced freshwater consumption while supporting manufacturing growth.

2008-2015

Renewable Energy Expansion

Large-scale renewable energy adoption.

Intel expanded renewable energy purchases and invested in cleaner electricity sources including wind and solar projects.

2016-2020

Greenhouse Gas Reduction

Lowering manufacturing emissions.

New technologies and efficiency upgrades helped reduce emissions while improving operational performance.

2021-2030

Net Positive Water

Restoring more water than consumed.

Intel works with environmental organizations and communities to restore watersheds and improve water availability.

2040

Net-Zero Emissions

Achieving carbon neutrality.

Intel's long-term goal is net-zero greenhouse gas emissions through innovation, renewable energy, and supply-chain collaboration.
